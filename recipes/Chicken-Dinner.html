Chicken Dinner Recipe

This delicious dish is perfect for when you're alone and empty inside!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz chicken breast
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 8 oz of brocoli
  • butter or oil for cooking
  • one-half of white onion

Steps

  1. put 1 cup of rice into a pan with 2 cups of water and cook
  2. heat up pan and coat lubricant of your choice
  3. cut up onion and garlic and sautee
  4. Insert chicken, cook until done
  5. CLEAN UP BOZO!
  6. Then Enjoy!
